**Section headline:** Why B.A.D. Works When Everything Else Hasn't

Your body isn't difficult. It's communicating.

Most programmes fail midlife women because they treat the body like a machine — input calories, output results. But your hormones, gut, nervous system and metabolism are all talking to each other. When one is struggling, the others feel it.

That's why "eating well" and "doing all the right things" still doesn't always work.

In B.A.D. Premium 1:1, we don't add more things to your list. We understand what your body is already telling us — then work with it, not against it.

**Three core principles:**

1. **Personalised to your physiology** — not a generic plan, not a group programme
2. **Supported daily** — not left to figure it out alone between sessions
3. **Grounded in science** — functional medicine, nutrition science, hormone health and lifestyle medicine

## FAQ Section

**Section headline:** Your Questions, Answered

---

**Q: What exactly is included in the membership?**

A: Everything you need for personalised, ongoing support — monthly 1:1 coaching call, daily WhatsApp food diary feedback, a personalised nutrition protocol, supplement protocol, monthly symptom tracking and plan adjustments, and access to community challenges and members-only content.

---

**Q: Do I need blood testing?**

A: Blood testing is optional but highly recommended — it allows us to build your plan around your actual biomarkers, not guesswork. You can add it at checkout or any time during your membership.

---

**Q: What if I've already tried lots of things and nothing has worked?**

A: That's exactly who this is designed for. If you've done "all the right things" and still don't feel right, it usually means the approach wasn't personalised enough. In B.A.D. Premium 1:1, we start by understanding your specific body — not applying a generic framework.

---

**Q: How much time does this take each week?**

A: It's designed to fit around your real life. The daily WhatsApp diary takes 2–3 minutes. The weekly check-in form takes around 5 minutes. Your monthly call is 30 minutes. Everything else happens in the background.

---

**Q: Can I pause my membership?**

A: Yes. One pause is allowed per 6-month period, for illness or holiday. Just let Monika know in advance.

---

**Q: What if I want to cancel?**

A: Monthly members can cancel with 30 days' notice at any time. Quarterly and annual members should refer to the terms at checkout.

---

**Q: Is this suitable for perimenopause?**

A: Yes — perimenopause and hormonal health are a core focus area. Monika works with many women navigating perimenopause, and the programme is built around the physiological changes of midlife.

---

**Q: How quickly will I see results?**

A: Results vary, and we don't make promises about specific outcomes. What most members notice within the first few weeks is that they feel more understood and supported, and that small changes begin to compound. Steady, sustainable shifts — not dramatic quick fixes.
