# Member Experience — B.A.D. Premium Health Coaching (1:1)

## Overview

This document describes what a B.A.D. Premium 1:1 member receives throughout their membership — daily, weekly, monthly and at key milestone points.

The experience is designed to feel supported without being overwhelming. Everything is personalised to the individual member's body, goals, and real life.

---

## Daily

| What | Detail |
|---|---|
| **WhatsApp food diary check-ins** | Member shares daily food diary via WhatsApp. Monika responds with personalised feedback, observations, and gentle adjustments. This is the core daily touchpoint. |
| **Direct messaging access** | Members can message Monika directly with questions, concerns, or anything that feels relevant. Responses during working hours. |

---

## Weekly

| What | Detail | GHL Automation |
|---|---|---|
| **Weekly check-in email** | Automated email with link to the weekly feedback form. Monika reviews responses before the next coaching interaction. | `1:1 Weekly Check-In` workflow, 7-day recurring trigger |
| **Consistency nudge** | Gentle prompt included in the check-in email to keep the member engaged and reflect on the week | Included in weekly check-in email |

**Weekly check-in form captures:**
- Energy levels (1–10)
- Digestion / gut symptoms
- Sleep quality
- Mood / stress levels
- Any notable changes or observations
- Questions for Monika

---

## Monthly (Day 25 Each Month)

| What | Detail | GHL Automation |
|---|---|---|
| **Monthly review email** | Sent on Day 25. Prompts member to complete the monthly check-in form and book their monthly 1:1 call. | `1:1 Monthly Review` workflow, date-based Day 25 trigger |
| **Monthly check-in form** | Deeper review of progress, symptoms, goals, and what to focus on next month | Linked in the monthly review email |
| **Monthly 1:1 coaching call (30 min)** | Video call with Monika. Monika reviews the check-in form in advance. Strategy session for the month ahead. | Booking link included in monthly review email |
| **Plan adjustments** | After the call, Monika updates the member's nutrition protocol, supplement recommendations, and focus areas as needed | Manual — Monika updates via membership area or GHL notes |

---

## Ongoing

| What | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Mini Membership access** | Members have full access to Monika's Mini Membership — weekly focus themes, practical tools, food ideas, light education, and community challenges. Introduced in Onboarding Email 2. |
| **Free challenges** | Access to all community challenges Monika runs — these are optional and designed to complement (not add to) the 1:1 work. |
| **Members-only content** | Exclusive educational content, protocols, and resources relevant to midlife health, hormones, gut, metabolism and lifestyle. |

---

## Month 2+ — Upsell Triggers

From Month 2 onwards, targeted upsell offers may be sent based on:
- Survey responses (symptoms still present, gut concerns, stress/energy issues)
- Observed patterns from check-ins
- Member's own flagging of specific concerns

> **Ethics rule:** No upsells sent before Month 1 is complete.

Full details: `docs/08-upsells-and-crosssells.md`

---

## Month 3 — Loyalty Retention Offer

At Month 3, members on the monthly plan receive a loyalty offer:

> "Continue for another 3 months at £150/month — saving £30/month."

**Trigger:** 90 days after `1:1 Active` tag applied
**Workflow name:** `1:1 Loyalty Retention — Month 3`
